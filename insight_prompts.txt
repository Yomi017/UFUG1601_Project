Prompt 1: Country Analysis
Goal: Analyze the social well-being of a specific country based on its indicators.
Prompt Template:
Analyze the social well-being of [Country Name] based on the following data:
Happiness Score: [Score]
GDP per Capita: [GDP]
Life Expectancy: [Life Expectancy]
Education Expenditure: [Edu Expenditure]
Provide 3 policy recommendations to maintain or improve this status. Focus on the relationship between education spending and happiness.

Prompt 2: Cluster Analysis
Goal: Interpret the characteristics of a specific cluster of countries.
Prompt Template:
I have performed K-Means clustering on global happiness data. One cluster has the following average characteristics:
Average Happiness Score: [Avg Score]
Average GDP per Capita: [Avg GDP]
Describe the likely socio-economic profile of countries in this cluster. What are the common challenges they might face?

Prompt 3: Global Trend Synthesis
Goal: Synthesize findings from the correlation analysis.
Prompt Template:
Based on a correlation analysis of global data, I found a strong positive correlation between GDP per Capita and Happiness Score (0.78), but a weaker correlation between Education Expenditure and Happiness (0.35). 
Explain why economic factors might have a more direct observed impact on happiness scores than education spending in the short term, while considering the long-term benefits of education.
